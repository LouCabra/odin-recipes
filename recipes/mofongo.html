Mofongo

This tasty dish serves 2-3 people. Prep time is 45 mins

Ingredients

  • 2 Chicken breasts- cut into 1" pieces
  • 2/3 cups of chopped Yellow onions
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • Salt
  • 1/2 cup of Cilatro
  • Fresh lime wedges
  • 1 tablespoon of Achiote paste
  • 2-3 tablespoon of the oil of your choice
  • 4 large Plantains - cut into 1" pieces
  • 1/2 tablespoon of Sea Salt
  • 4-5 Stalks of Green oions- chopped very fine
  • 2-3 cloves of Garlic- minced
  • 1/4 cup of Olive oil
  • 3 cups of the frying oil (your choice)

  1. STEP

    2. Start by heating up some oil in a wide skillet.

  2. STEP

    3. Add the chopped up onions and let them cook for 4-5 mins on med hig heat until they are translucent. You can now add the achiote paste and stir it around for a min, I should mention here that I used half the amount of paste for this recipe, which was roughly 3 Tbs.

  3. STEP

    4. Add the chicken to the skillet and after about 2 mins add the tomatoes. Season with some salt. Add the bell peppers during the last 3 mins of cooking. Let it cook uncovered on med high heat for 12- 15 mins or until the chicken is done. Set aside.

  4. STEP

    5. While the chicken is cooking you can start working on the plantains. Deep fry the plantain pieces until they are light golden brown for about 3-4 mins. You don't want to make them very crunchy the inside should remain mushy. Alternatively you could also steam the plantains for a lighter variation.

  5. STEP

    6. Mash the plantains like you would potatoes. And then add garlic, green onions and some olive oil to the mashed plantains. Season with some salt.

  6. FINAL STEP

    7. Form molds on a plate with your hands and fill it up with the chicken filling. Sprinkle some fresh cilantro on top and serve with a wedge of lime. Buen provecho!
